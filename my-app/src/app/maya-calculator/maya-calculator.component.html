Index Investing A Low-Cost, Diversified, and Easy Way to Grow Your Money Index investing is a passive investment strategy that seeks to replicate the returns of a benchmark index. Index funds are baskets of securities that track a specific index, such as the S&P 500 or the Dow Jones Industrial Average. Index funds are typically much cheaper than actively managed funds, and they offer a simple and easy way to invest in the market.
